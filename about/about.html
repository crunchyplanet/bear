Hi there!

I'm Jinghan Luo. I also go by Jillian. I'm an interaction designer based in San Francisco, currently looking for an internship in summer 2019. I have a background in Psychology and now I'm pursuing a master's degree in Design at California College of the Arts.

My passion and practice range widely from interaction design, motion design, to visual design and animation. I enjoy using code and physical materials to realize my designs. On the side, I love photography and illustration.

My work explores different ways of human-machine interaction. I look for the fun and delight in people's experience in my design. I'm interested in human communication, information technology and entertainment.
