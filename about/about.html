Hi there!

I'm Jinghan Luo. I also go by Jillian. I'm an interaction designer based in San Francisco, currently looking for an internship in summer 2019. With a background in psychology, I'm now pursuing a master's degree in design with a focus on interaction and graphic design at California College of the Arts.

My passion and practice range widely across design disciplines -- interaction, motion, visual and animation. I enjoy coding and I consider it a valuable tool in my work. I also dabble in photography and illustration for fun.

My work explores different ways human interact with technology. I look for the fun and delight in the user experience I create. I'm passionate about the nature and new technologies.
