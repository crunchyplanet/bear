Hi there!

I'm Jinghan Luo. I also go by Jillian. I'm an interaction designer based in San Francisco, currently looking for an internship in summer 2019. With a background in psychology, I'm now pursuing a master's degree in the interdisciplinary design program at California College of the Arts, with a focus in interaction design.

My passion and practice grow in many design disciplines. User experience, visual design, motion design and animation all spark joy for me. I am also an active learner in coding and I consider it a very valuable tool in my work.

I like to explore different ways human interact with technology. And I look for the fun and delight in the user experience I create.

Aside from design, I also dabble in photography and illustration. I love nature and wild life. My dream is going on an Antarctica expedition and wobble with the waddles.
