Hi, I'm Jillian.

I'm an interaction designer, currently studying in the interdisciplinary design program at California College of the Arts, with a focus in interaction design. I'll be receiving my MFA degree in design in a few months and I'm seeking a UX/product design position.

As a designer, I like to explore ways to create compelling visual and interactive experiences, especially with the involvement of motion, storytelling, and humor.
With an academic background in psychology, I’m attentive to how people perceive the world around them, how they process information, what alters their behavior, and what triggers their emotions in an experience.

As a nature person, I seek opportunities to make people become better inhabitants of this planet, learn to live in peace with it, while being a curious explorer.
With a passion for science and technology, I look for ways to humanize technical systems and utilize them to facilitate and enrich people’s lives.
