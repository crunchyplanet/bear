Hello, I'm Jillian.
A product designer based in San Francisco.
My Background
I graduated from California College of the Arts in 2020 with an MFA degree in interaction design, recently completed a UX design internship at Cincom Systems, where I gained a lot of experience solving complex problems in business-facing applications.
My Passion in Design
There are many different titles for the job in which we design products and services and make sure they're easy to use. Whether I call myself a UX designer, product designer, or interaction designer, my passion lies in building delightful experiences that solve problems and bring positive impact.
My Interests

With a background in psychology, I’m attentive to how people perceive the world around them, how we process information, what alters our behavior, and what triggers our emotions in an experience.
Read about my research on environmental psychology for a design project ⇢

As a nature person, I seek opportunities to make people become better inhabitants of this planet, learn to live in peace with it, while being a curious explorer.

With a passion for science and technology, I look for ways to humanize technical systems and utilize them to facilitate and enrich people’s lives.
