En Route

2019
Individual Project
Product Design | UX/UI
Adobe XD · Figma · Illustrator · After Effects · Premiere

En Route is a tool that makes travel planning and route tracking easy and enjoyable. With maximum customizability, the user will be able to plan a route that covers any important spot they wish to visit, along with other activities that meet their interests. When the user is on their trip, the app will manage real-time updates according to where they are on their route, and provide insider tips and reminders along the way.

RESEARCH & IDEATION

The target user is travelers who want an easier way to plan their own trips. They might have some interests and priorities in mind as well as some restrictions around time and budget. And they want to make practical and informed travel plans and get the most out of their experience.

Based on my idea of the target user, I created a proto-persona and a journey map to identify the pain points and design opportunities.

Through brainstorm, I came up with some features that would help the user make a plan without painstaking research and arrangement but still meet their interests and restrictions. To make the most of the plan, I also thought about how it will come into play during the trip and what would help with its flexibility.

SKETCH & PROTOTYPE

I decided to build a tool that facilitates people to make highly customizable plans according to their preferences, which is enabled by a strong knowledge base and algorithms. While people are traveling, it would serve as an informed guide for the traveler to refer to at any time. I focused on designing the experience of making the plan and following the route during the trip.

In the planning phase, the traveler's needs are broken down to different parameters, and the user could be as specific as they wish about their preferences. A suggested route will be generated based on their input. Each spot on the route can be edited so the user could explore different options while keeping the spots that they like.

During the trip, the user can get information and tips about destinations. Some steps or destinations will also allow users to jump to other relevant apps like Uber, Airbnb, and Yelp for extented functions. If a change to the plan comes up, or an unexpected situation like a bad weather is detected, the app will offer suggestions to adjust the plan.

In addition to the mobile app, I also designed a web version mainly for the planning phase, so that the user could have more space to view the preference choices for the trip.

ANIMATION & MOTION DESIGN

I created some animation to fill in the transition between the user's input and the system-generated route to give the user a better idea of what's been taken into consideration.

I also designed animation that displays when the user reload for a different result. Having the extra time between the input and output not only allows time for the algorithm to calculate in a technical sense, but also communicates to the user that the system is treating every demand carefully.

Lessons Learned

• Different touchpoints (devices) could be used to cater to the needs of the user in different phases of their experience. Using the web platform for information-heavy scenarios and mobile version for quick data retrival could make it more efficient and easier to maneuver for the user.

• When designing the user interface for collecting the user's input, the input method(typing, selecting, sliding bar, etc.) should match the type of information, helping the user to understand the indication of their response and avoiding confusions.
