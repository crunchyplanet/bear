KNOWIE
Your All-around Personal Consultant

2019

Individual Project

Product Design | UX/UI

Knowie is a crowdsourcing Q&A platform that enables people with experience and expertise in different areas to answer each other's questions by directly communicating with each other. Knowie comes in the form of a desktop device and a mobile app, allowing the user to seek consultation and provide help at any time and place.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The massiveness of online resources has made information acquisition really easy. There is a variety of platforms that cater to our needs for knowledge, advice, and recommendations. The search bar has become our best buddy when it comes to consultation and problem-solving. Especially with crowdsourcing, we're able to reach out broadly and learn from other people's experience and expertise.

However, the large amount of information could sometimes be overwhelming. It is a hassle to sort through all the sources and extract the most useful piece. Even with platforms dedicated to questions and answers, sometimes you need some luck to get up-to-date solutions that apply to your particular case without too much digging. How might we make the most of crowdsourcing and exchange experience and expertise in an efficient way? How might we reshape the experience of consulting the internet and make it feel personal?

RESEARCH

I started by creating a persona for the user group and identifying the frustrations and needs of the user.

Keeping the user's needs in mind, I researched some existing crowdsourcing Q&A platforms such as Quora and Stackoverflow and studied their features. I was also referenced to an older search service called Chacha, which provided answers to people's individual questions over text. The research gave me a better idea of how to foster a knowledge exchange dynamic and what I should focus on to keep the experience simple and rewarding.

IDEATION

Through brainstorming, I defined the important and features of the platform I'm designing. The platform could be accessed from both a mobile app when you're on the go and from a destop device when you're at home or in the office. Each question will be directed to a specific person who has experience or expertise in the matter and provide helpful answers. The person who asks the answer will be able to follow up more easily due to the conversational nature of this exchange.

The platform would be a great place to seek help and advice for a broad range of issues. Especially when the user needs some personalized consultation or when the user has a question that's more specific and hard to find direct answer to on the internet. It's also good for situations where the user only needs a quick answer but has a hard time digging through the search result or finding the appropriate search term. An experienced person will be able to provide a quick pointer.

Storyboard

After developing the initial concept, I continued to think about ways to make the personified character(I named it Knowie, a know-it-all, but a helpful and approachable one) of the platform smarter and spontaneous. The reason for there to be a representative character is it will make the communication more natural and reassuring. Knowie will be the bridge between the questioner and the answerer. It will also provide suggestions and assistance along the side and make the user's experience more smooth. This would include helping clarify the question, suggest possible questions that the user might have based on their interests and recent activities. When a user receives an answer, Knowie could also help understand and interpret it, and provide directions.

PROTOTYPING

The Knowie mobile app will allow the user to search and send questions, have conversations with other users, and exchange virtual gifts. The desktop device will carry a simplified version of the features and functions to make the interaction succinct and efficient.

Landing Page
The landing page allows the first time users ask their questions directly without having to go through a tedious onboarding process.

Selecting Your Areas
If you're a new user, Knowie will prompt you to select the areas that you know well and feel comfortable answering questions in. As you do more searches and answer more questions, Knowie will learn your interests and domains and suggest them to you.

Billboard
The billboard is the main page in the app. Users could enter questions, view the popular topics of the day, see answer requests, and join other users' ongoing conversations.

Search
When the user searches for a question, Knowie will first gather results from the past conversations about similar topics, only including the relatively recent results. If the user sees someone's past answers and think they might be able to help, the user could long-press on their picture to start a conversation with them. If past results don't seem to be helpful at all, the user could simply send out their question and start a new conversation.

Receiving A Question
When a user receives a question but for some reason couldn't answer, they could deflect the question. If they don't know the answer and would like to follow the question, they also have an option to do so.

Conversations
All conversations that the user is having or had before are listed in the "conversations" tab for easy tracking.

Knowie's Assistance
Knowie will do its best to provide assistance during the discussion, connecting the dots by helping specify the questions, locating the mentioned resources, suggesting directions for further exploration, etc.

Conclude A Question
When a discussion comes to an end, the user could conclude the question. If it didn't help, the user could move on to asking other people having the previous conversation in mind. They could even choose to let the new person who receives the question view the conversation they already had, so that they could have some context and target the right focus more easily.

Highlighting the Useful Information
When a user concludes a question, they'll be prompted to highlight the most useful portion of the conversation so that Knowie will have a better idea about what's the main takeaway and provide a more representative preview for people who come across this record later.

Sending Gifts and Thank-you Notes
After a fruitful discussion, the user who asked the question will have the choice to send a virtual gift and a piece of note to the user who answered the question. There are designed default gifts to choose from. But customization is also encouraged. People could come up with their own idea for gifts and Knowie will generate a well-illustrated image to be attached to the message. This gesture will add more fun and personality to the knowledge exchange and make people enjoy giving and receiving appreciation more.

Profile Page
On the profile page, the user could view and manage their selected fields and see their collection of gifts. The fields in which the user has endorsed answers or responded a lot will be displayed under the corresponding marks. There is also a switch that allows the user to indicate their availabilty, which is also present as a physical toggle on Knowie Desktop.
