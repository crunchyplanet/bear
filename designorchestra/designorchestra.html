Design Orchestra

2019

In collaboration with Mitch Greer & Qian Zeng

Experience Design | Branding

Design Orchestra is a web-based visual + musical experience for a group of people to compose themed sound pieces and perform them by playing with the visual layout of pictorial notations.

Interface Design

The underlying story arc o the composed piece consists of three scenes, the forest, the city and the beach. We designed the notations that people use to compose and play to be illustrations that represent different sound sources in these three scenes, such as birds, storefront, and lighthouse.

We built a browser-based system and set up touchscreens so people could easily interact with it. I was responsible for the composing page, which I coded using p5.js. During the experience, the assigned "composers" first place the illustrated icons on the lines, which are mapped to a three-minute sequence.

When people start playing, the lines and background on the page will be animated and the icons will appear in order. The performers will each be in charge of one sound, when they see their corresponding icon showing on the screen, they would press the button on their individual screen to trigger the sound. Collaboratively, the participants create a sound journey with visual elements.

To create a complete experience, we also designed tickets and playbills for the event.
